\chapter{Experiment}
\label{ch:experiment}

Because doctors quickly gave us positive feedback to the algorithms usefulness, we conducted an experiment probing doctors intuitions about similarities between pairs of physician letters.
We found that their ratings correlate strongly with the distance measures our algorithm provides for letter pairs.
